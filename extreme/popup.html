Site Cusomizations

The format is url 00100. The url part is the URL, and the numbers correspond to images, scripts, fonts, media, and ads respectively. 1 means do nor block and 0 means block. Example: https://www.google.com 01100 blocks images, media and ads on the Google homepage.
